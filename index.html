MY STORY

Most people see AI as a way to replace human effort. I see it as a way to amplify human flourishing. As the Program Manager for the AI Lab at Biola University, I don't just automate processes; I build the environments where people and technology solve high-stakes problems together. I operate at the intersection of High-Level Design (HLD) and Human Agency, ensuring that every system I deploy makes the person using it look better, enjoy their work, and deliver higher-quality results.

I believe the best way to bridge the gap between technical potential and business reality is through immediate evidence. When stakeholders are hesitant about AI adoption, I don't give them a slide deck; I give them an MVP. By showing results right away, I help stakeholders visualize the trade-offs of their time and feel empowered to invest in the core business. For example, by teaching non-technical teams the basics of AI and data protection, I've successfully transitioned departments from manual, 300-hour processes to balanced, human-in-the-loop systems.

My philosophy is centered on unblocking the person to increase organizational velocity. I've realized that most operational bottlenecks are actually misalignments in vision rather than technical failures. I solve this by working simultaneously from a top-down and bottoms-up perspective to ensure everyone lands in the same place. This involves rooting every initiative in the company's core mission while deeply understanding what individual team members care about. By finding the middle ground where personal growth intersects with the project's success, I connect the right people to the right resources and create a flat hierarchy of high-agency individuals who can execute with little to no oversight.

I am always looking to learn from others who are navigating the complexities of people and systems. Whether you are actively building a team or just want to make a new friend, I'm always open to a conversation. My goal is to stay connected with those who view growth as a mission, and I'm happy to be a resource for anyone looking to help their people and their business reach their fullest potential.
